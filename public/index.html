Terms Connected to Mythology

  • Venus/Aphrodite
    • Venus = love, beauty, desire, sex, fertility, prosperity, and victory
    • mother of Aeneas and Julius Caesar said she was an ancestor of his
    • Aphrodite = love, beauty, pleasure, and procreation
    • Aphrodisia = annual festival held in Greece to honor her
    • dove = sacred bird of Aphrodite
    • married to Hephaestus but had many lovers
  • Vulcan/Hephaestus
    • Hephaestus = blacksmith, metalworking, carpenters, craftsmen, artisans, sculptors, metallurgy, fire, and volcanoes.
  • Mars/Ares
  • Cupid/Eros
  • Cupid and Psyche
  • Juno/Hera
  • Jupiter/Zeus
  • Some of the Loves of Zeus: Leto, Leda, Europa, Metis, Mnemosyne, and Io
  • Athena; Aegis
  • Castor and Pollux
